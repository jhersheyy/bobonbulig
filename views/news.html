News & Events

"The smallest act of kindness is worth more than the grandest intention"

Aid to "Ruby/ Hagupit" Disaster

Due to the recent typhoon "Ruby/Hagupit" Bulig Foundation is trying to coordinate aid and assistance to the people who have suffered damages to their homes and properties. We welcome any assistance you can give to the Foundation.

Become a Volunteer

We seek for volunteers and do Fundraising events to fund community programs. Once or twice a year Bulig Foundation organizes a fundraising event to be able to fund community programs that the foundation advocates for.

Health and Wellness Awareness Mission

Bulig Foundation conducts wellness missions in underserved communities to encourage and educate individuals and families how to live a healthy lifestyle.
