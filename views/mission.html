Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To assist in education, uplift Social, Economic, Health and living condition of individuals and families in underserved and impoverished communities

Bulig Foundation conducts wellness missions in underserved communities to encourage and educate individuals and families how to live a healthy lifestyle.

Our Vision

To make a difference in the lives of individuals and families one person or family at a time.
