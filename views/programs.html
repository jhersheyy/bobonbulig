Community Programs

Uplift Social and Economic Condition

Poverty is undeniably the most critical social problem that affects most impoverished countries all over the world. It is usually caused by a variety of factors such as lack of quality education, lack of jobs, low economic growth, high inflation, natural disasters, over population, corruption and political instability. The Philippines is a country that exemplifies the correlation between these factors and poverty. About 27% of Filipinos still live below the poverty line and while according to World Bank the number of people living in extreme poverty has dropped in every other developing region in the world.Other surveys say that some Filipinos are still living on less than $1.25 per day and in order for a family to escape poverty they need to earn about P2,000.00 pesos or $48.00 a month! Bulig Foundation will encourage individuals and families to be self-sufficient by helping them find or create sustainable jobs. The foundation will explore programs that are proven to be helpful in uplifting the economic conditions in the poor communities. Bulig Foundation will make the programs available to poor individuals who show desire and willingness to work.The program will also be available to the impoverished and underserved families and communities both locally and globally

Medical, Surgical, Dental and Wellness Missions

It is a sad reality, but a large part of the population in some third world countries still live in poverty. There is still inequality when it comes to accessibility to good healthcare and dental care. Bulig Foundation hopes to help people who are living in these impoverished communities to have a chance to live a better and healthier lives through medical, surgical, dental and wellness missions. The services will be performed or delivered by volunteer healthcare professionals like doctors, nurses, dentists, physical therapists, respiratory therapists, and other healthcare and non-healthcare volunteer professionals

Financial assistance in education

Unfortunately, there are still a large portion of the population who are unable to afford to send children to school therefore perpetuating the cycle of poverty. In most third world countries insufficient funding is the biggest reason why children drop out of school. In some cases they are pulled out of school by their own parents so they can help the family earn extra money to buy food, clothing and other necessities. Bulig Foundation helps these individuals and families by offering them financial assistance in education so children can stay in school instead of in the fields.

Provide aid and relief to victims of disasters and unforeseen catastrophic event

Disasters are unpredictable and can threaten lives, damage properties and affect the social and economic condition of individuals and families. Bulig Foundation would like to be able to help people and communities who are stricken by disasters, typhoons, flooding, fire, earthquakes, etc. The foundation will assist in the repair and restoration of essential services like medical, social and educational services. The foundation, its members, affiliates and volunteers will try to help the victims and the community as a whole and hope to be able to provide appropriate assistance to the community. Assistance may be provided in many ways, it can be monetary, in kind, medical or service.
